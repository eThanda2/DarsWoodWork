Meet Dara

Welcome to The Dara Woodshop, proudly based in The Woodlands, Texas. As a female entrepreneur in the woodworking industry, I specialize in building custom gaming TV centers, transforming old tables into stunning modern desks, and crafting bespoke furniture using pallet wood and plywood.

From the initial design to the final finishing touches, I pour my passion into creating highly functional, beautifully crafted pieces that elevate your space.
